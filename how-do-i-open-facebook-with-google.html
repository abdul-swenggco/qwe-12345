A StepbyStep Guide to Opening Facebook via Google

To open Facebook via Google, follow these step-by-step instructions. Start by launching your web browser and navigating to the Google homepage. In the search bar, type "Facebook" and hit enter. The first search result should be the official Facebook website. Click on it to proceed.

Once you are on the Facebook homepage, you will see two empty boxes labeled "Email or Phone" and "Password." This is where you enter your login credentials. If you already have a Facebook account, fill in your email or phone number and password in the respective fields. If you don't have an account yet, click on the "Create New Account" link below the login form to sign up for a new Facebook account. After entering your login information, click on the "Log In" button to access your Facebook account.
